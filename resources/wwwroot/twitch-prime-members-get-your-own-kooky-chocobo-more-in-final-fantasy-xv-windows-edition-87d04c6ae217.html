Twitch Prime members, Get Your Own Kooky Chocobo & More in Final Fantasy XV: Windows Edition!

Final Fantasy XV Windows Edition has finally arrived! Twitch Prime Members will be able to claim the Kooky Chocobo and 10,000 Gil to start their ultimate journey off right. We're just getting started, so be on the lookout for more rewards in the coming months. In the meantime, join your closest friends on the road trip of a lifetime through a breathtaking open world in ultimate quality in Final Fantasy XV Windows Edition.

Redemption Instructions:

Not a Twitch Prime member?
1. Visit twitchprime.com and sign up for a free 30 day trial
2. Launch Final Fantasy XV Windows Edition and click on “Special Gift From Twitch Prime” in the main menu
3. Log in and link your Twitch Account
4. Click the Crown Icon on Twitch.tv and click “Claim Offer” on the Final Fantasy XV Loot
5. Restart the game to collect your special gift!

Existing Twitch Prime member?
1. Launch Final Fantasy XV Windows Edition and click on “Special Gift From Twitch Prime” in the main menu
2. Log in and link your Twitch Account
3. Click the Crown Icon on Twitch.tv and click “Claim Offer” on the Final Fantasy XV Loot
4. Restart the game to collect your special gift!
Getting your in-game loot:
- Your 10,000 Gil will be available in the Main Menu after completion of the initial cutscenes
- To access your Kooky Chocobo skin you will need to reach Chapter 3 and navigate to the Chocobo post in-game and customize your Chocobo.
